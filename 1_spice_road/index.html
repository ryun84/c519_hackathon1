Welcome to Spice Road!

Acquire as many victory points as possible by purchasing Victory Cards.
Each Victory Card costs one yellow spice and awards Victory Points.
Use Merchant Cards to acquire spices.
Once a player attains 5 Victory Cards, the other player will have one more turn to gather as many points as possible.
The winner is the player with the most victory points.
Good luck and have fun!
