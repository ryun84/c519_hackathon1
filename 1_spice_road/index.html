Welcome to Spice Road!

The goal of this game is to acquire as many victory points as possible. This can be done by purchasing victory/points cards you see at the top of the screen. The cost of each victory/points card is one yellow spice. Spices can be acquired/traded by selecting merchant cards in the second row and adding them to your player's hand. Click a card in your hand to use it and activate its effects. Each player gets one action (mouse click) per turn. Once a player attains 5 victory/points cards, the other player(s) will have one more turn/action to attain points. Once all final turns have ended, the winner will be determined by the player with the most victory points. Good luck and have fun!
